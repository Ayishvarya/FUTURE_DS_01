# Business Sales Dashboard from E-commerce data 

This repository features a comprehensive suite of interactive dashboards designed to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) across sales, profitability, and regional logistics. By visualizing multidimensional data, this project provides a 360-degree view of business health and operational efficiency.

---

##  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks the following core metrics to provide instant business health snapshots:
* **Total Sales:** $2.30M across all regions.
* **Total Customers:** High engagement with 793 unique customers.
* **Total Quantity:** 38K units shipped globally.
* **Profitability:** Analyzing the direct impact of discounts on bottom-line margins.

##  Tech Stack
* **Visualization Tool:** Power BI 
* **Data Processing:** SQL 
* **Analytics Focus:** Trend Analysis, Regional Distribution, and Shipping Efficiency

---

## Visual Insights & Analysis

### 1. Regional Sales Distribution
The **Sales by Region** analysis shows a balanced market presence with the **West** region leading at 31.58% ($725.46K) and the **East** following closely at 29.55%.

### 2. Logistics & Shipping Modes
Operational data reveals that **Standard Class** is the dominant shipping mode, accounting for approximately 59.72% of all orders. This insight helps in negotiating better freight rates with standard carriers.

### 3. Profit vs. Discount Correlation
A critical visualization in this project is the **Profit by Discount** line graph. 
* **Insight:** There is a sharp decline in profitability as discount rates exceed 0.2 (20%). 
* **Action:** This suggests a need for stricter discount caps to protect profit margins.

### 4. Sales Seasonality 
The **Total Sales by Year** and **Quantity by Month** charts highlight significant spikes in the latter half of the year, particularly around September. 
* **Finding:** Volume increases steadily from June to September, indicating strong Q3 performance.

---

## Strategic Recommendations
Based on the dashboard insights, the following actions are recommended:
1.  **Optimization:** Focus marketing efforts on the **West and East regions**, which combined contribute over 60% of total revenue.
2.  **Profit Protection:** Re-evaluate the discount strategy for products showing negative profit trends at higher discount tiers.
3.  **Inventory Planning:** Scale up inventory and staffing in anticipation of the Q3 volume surge identified in the monthly quantity trends.
